ABOUT US

CandS, Consulting and Service, was founded to solve an ever-present issue plaguing the industry: the over-complexification of basic tasks. Over the years, we've been on top of that problem, streamlining our clients' infrastructure and providing them with the tools to boost their efficiency tenfold.

Our team boasts an impressive 50 years of collective experience in software development, and with the regular acquisition of new and exciting talent, we always have the best crew for the task at hand.

We aim to exponentially improve the efficiency of your company, solving issues before they become problems and reducing the complexity of your every task. To that end, we've developed tools such as our in-house multiplatform app, Score! Allowing our clients to monitor their data, order stock automatically, manage the routes and loads of delivery trucks, and even notify their suppliers so that the exact amounts of raw material are ready when they need to be.
